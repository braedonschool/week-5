France During WWII

Pre-War

Back in the early months of World War II, one of the Germans first targets was France, due to Frances stalwart battle against Germany in the first World War, they wanted revenge.

The French, since WWI had ended had established the Maginot line, a heavy line of defenses at the north eastern border of France, as pictured above. The had left the border of Belgium and Luxembourg fairly under-protected, due to dense terrain there, though to be impassable.
